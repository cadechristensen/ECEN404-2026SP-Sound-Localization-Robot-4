UART Protocol (Pi <-> ESP32)
============================

Pi -> ESP32:
    "NAV angle=<float> dist_ft=<float>\n"
    angle: bearing in degrees (positive=right, negative=left)
    dist_ft: distance to sound source in feet

ESP32 -> Pi:
    "READY\n"    — arrived at target
    "OBSTACLE\n" — obstacle detected, ESP32 handling avoidance
    "RELISTEN\n" — dead end or deadlock, need re-localization

Baud: 115200
Port: /dev/serial0 (Pi), UART1 tx=17 rx=16 (ESP32)
